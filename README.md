# FlightDesk AI

## AI-Powered Flight School Management Platform

FlightDesk AI is a cloud-based platform designed for flight schools, flying clubs, and aviation training organizations.

The platform helps manage aircraft, maintenance, bookings, instructors, students, and AI-powered assistance in one integrated system.

---

## Vision

Build the most intuitive AI-powered management platform for flight schools in Europe.

---

## Main Modules

- Dashboard
- Aircraft Management
- Maintenance Tracking
- Flight Booking
- Student Management
- Instructor Management
- AI Assistant
- Reports
- Notifications
