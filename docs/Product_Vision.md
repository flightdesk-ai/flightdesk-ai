# FlightDesk AI

## Product Vision

### Mission

To simplify flight school management by combining aircraft scheduling, maintenance tracking, student management, and AI-powered assistants into one cloud platform.

---

## Problem

Most flight schools still use:

- Excel spreadsheets
- Google Calendar
- WhatsApp
- Phone calls
- Paper logbooks

This causes:

- double bookings
- maintenance conflicts
- poor aircraft utilization
- manual paperwork
- communication problems
- lack of real-time information

---

## Solution

FlightDesk AI provides one integrated platform where administrators, instructors, students and maintenance personnel work together.

The platform includes:

- Aircraft management
- Aircraft booking
- Maintenance tracking
- Student portal
- Instructor portal
- AI Assistant
- Notifications
- Reports

---

## Target Customers

- Flight Schools (ATO)
- DTO
- Flying Clubs
- Aero Clubs

---

## Long-term Vision

Become the leading AI platform for flight training management in Europe.
