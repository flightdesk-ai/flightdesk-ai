# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose

This document defines the functional and non-functional requirements for FlightDesk School, the first product of the FlightDesk Platform.

It serves as the primary reference for product planning, software architecture, database design, UI/UX development, AI integration and future platform expansion.

---

# 2. Vision

FlightDesk Platform is an AI-powered Digital Operating System for Flight Schools.

The platform centralizes aircraft management, maintenance planning, scheduling, training management and operational decision-making into one cloud-based ecosystem.

---

# 3. Mission

Simplify flight school operations by replacing disconnected spreadsheets, paper records and manual coordination with one intelligent platform.

---

# 4. Business Goals

The first commercial release must:

* Reduce scheduling conflicts
* Prevent maintenance-related aircraft downtime
* Improve aircraft utilization
* Simplify instructor scheduling
* Improve operational visibility
* Reduce administrative workload
* Prepare flight schools for future AI automation

# 7. Business Problems

Current industry problems include:

* Scheduling conflicts
* Maintenance coordination failures
* Instructor workload imbalance
* Student progress fragmentation
* Lack of operational visibility
* Audit preparation complexity
* Communication breakdowns
* Resource bottlenecks
* Manual administration
* Limited scalability

---

# 8. Product Philosophy

FlightDesk is not simply a booking application.

FlightDesk is a Digital Operating System for Flight Schools.

Every aircraft, instructor, student and maintenance event exists inside one integrated operational model.
